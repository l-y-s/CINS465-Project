How to play

For those already familiar with text-based games and/or MUDs, you can select the Play option from the navigation menu above to jump right into the game.
For beginners, here are a couple helpful tips:

  • Choose a memorable name, you won't be able to change it later!
  • All actions you wish to perform will be typed into the prompt, generally of the form [verb] [noun] (e.g. get key). There is a little flexibility in the formation of commands, so don't worry too much about having very well formed commands.
  • Basic descriptions of your surroundings will be presented when you enter a new area, and they can be repeated by using the "look" command. Objects and players in the world will also have descriptions and hints. Be sure to "look" at anything and everything in the game world, you may discover additional details you didn't know existed!
  • Be sure to try out the tutorial module before jumping into the game proper. The intro room at the beginning of the game will help you get started.
  • And finally, you can use the command "help" to get a short list of available commands at any point.

Good luck, and have fun!
